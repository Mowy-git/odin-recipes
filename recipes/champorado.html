Champorado

This champorado recipe is a hearty sweet rice (malagkit) with chocolate that is great for snacking.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup glutinous sweet rice
  • 2 cups light coconut milk
  • Â½ cup cocoa powder
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup thick coconut milk

Directions

Step 1
Combine rice and light coconut milk in a large pot; bring to a boil for 10 minutes, while stirring occasionally to keep rice from sticking to the bottom of the pot.

Step 2
Stir cocoa powder, sugar, and salt into rice; reduce heat to low, cover, and continue cooking, stirring occasionally, until rice is tender, about 10 minutes

Step 3
Pour thick coconut milk into rice mixture; stir to combine. Serve hot.
